B.Sc. (Hons.) Community Science

Course Name:

B.Sc (Hons) in Community Science
[Previously known as B.Sc Home Science]

Duration:

4 Years (8 Semesters)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Stream Requirements: PCB, PCM, or Arts streams eligibility.
  • Minimum Marks: 50% marks required for General Category.
  • Admission Through:
    • CUET (UG)
    • University Entrance Exam

Course Overview

Community Science is a multidisciplinary course that focuses on improving the quality of life of individuals, families, and communities.

What Students Study About:

  • Human Nutrition
  • Food Science
  • Child Development
  • Resource Management & Consumer Science
  • Textile & Fashion
  • Extension Education & Communication Management

Is This Course For You?

This path is excellent if you have a keen interest or love for:

  • Biology & Health
  • Working with Nutrition & Wellness
  • Child Development
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Creating Social Impact

Skills for Best Placement

1. Management & Designing Tools

  • Auto CAD: Learn this software to design house maps and plan beautiful interior spaces efficiently.

2. Nutrition & Lab Skills

  • Diet Planning: Utilize software and structured charts to accurately calculate calories and plan customized, specific diets for sick patients.
  • Food Preservation: Master chemical and natural methodologies to freeze, dry, and safely pack food products to extend shelf life and prevent spoilage.

3. Media & Communication Skills

  • Audio-Visual Editing
  • Psychological Testing

4. Textile & Fashion Skills

  • Garment Construction
  • Textile Testing

5. Digital & Content Skills

  • Social Media Marketing
  • UI/UX Design

6. Professional Business Skills

  • Data Analysis
  • Grant Writing

Government Job Opportunities

  • Gov Health & Awareness Programme: Mass Media Officer, Health Educator.
  • Ministry of Agriculture & Rural Development: Extension Officer, Krishi Vigyan Kendra Scientist.
  • Ministry of Information & Broadcasting: Gov Journalist / Reporter, Public Relations Officer.
  • Resource Management & Consumer Affairs: Consumer Affairs Department positions, Consumer Protection Officer, District Consumer Forum Executive.
  • Estate & Event Management: Estate Officer / Property Manager, Gov Event Manager.
  • Food & Nutrition Roles: Food Safety Officer, Government Dietitians.
  • Sports Departments: Sports Nutritionist roles within the Sports Authority of India (SAI).
  • Food Corporation of India (FCI): Technical Officer posts.
  • Research Institutions: Research Assistant positions in prominent bodies like NCERT, ICMR, and NIPCCD (National Institute of Public Cooperation and Child Development).
  • Higher Education: Opportunities to work as an Assistant Professor or Lecturer.
  • Handicrafts & Textiles: Handicrafts / Textile Marketing Officer, Designer positions in Weaver's Service Centres.
  • Welfare Services: CDPO (Child Development Project Officer) in government departments or allied NGOs.
