Can You Start Your Own Business?

Yes, This course is considered highly beneficial for entrepreneurship .After completing it you can work on the business pathways listed below:

  • Nutrition & Diet Consulting: You can open your own clinical diet and nutrition clinic.
  • Child Care & Day-Care Centres: You can establish a school or activity center based on early childhood development standards.
  • Food and Nutritional Startups: Safe health packed foods or organic production line.
  • Boutique & Textile Business: Fashion design consulting and garments merchandising setups.
  • Wellness Coaching: On Online platforms personalized life-improvement models.
